Before You Order

A few honest words from our kitchen.

01

These are home-made products, prepared fresh in small batches — not factory-sealed. Treat them as you would food from your own kitchen.

02

We use no preservatives of any kind. Please enjoy them fresh, store cool, and finish within the suggested days.

03

Always check the ingredient label on your pack before eating, especially if you are sensitive to nuts, dairy, gluten or soya.

04

If you are allergic to any ingredient, tell us in the "Special Requirements" box at checkout — we'll do our best to make your batch without it.
